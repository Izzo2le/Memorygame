Instructions



Flip over 2 cards and look to see if they are a match


If the cards do not match, try clicking on two new cards.


If the cards match they will stay face up for you to see throughout the game, you will see your score card show you how many pairs are left to find.


You can keep playing until all of the cards are face up.


Once all the cards have been overturned, you can start the game again.
